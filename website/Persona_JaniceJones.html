  3. Janice Jones

Janice is a doctor and the mother of two children. She has been living in Camden for 3 years and both of her children are students working very hard on their A-Level exam. Recently, there has been a lot noise coming from her neighbour at night, which makes her children cannot concentrate on revising and did not have enough sleep at night. Due to the consistent noise coming from her neighbours, Janice and her children has been experiencing insomnia and mental strain. Janice feels the situation has gone worse than before and will definitely have an impact on their daily lives. However, she does not know what can she do about it or who to reach out for. Since she is very busy at work during the day, she does not have time to visit the council or calling them as the Camden Council office hours clash with her own working hours. She is very worry about this condition and hoping to find a way to stop this situation from continuing.

Motivation & Goals
  • Support her children
  • Help the local community and be involved in charitable events
