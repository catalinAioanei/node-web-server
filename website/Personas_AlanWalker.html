Alan Walker

  3. Alan Walker

Alan is a 27 year old banker and has been working in the finance sector for 3 years now. He is currently living in Victoria and wishes to move in to Camden as he is working at a bank in Camden. He is looking for buying an apartment in Camden. He sees buying an apartment as an investment, which means the future development of this area should be considered in order to know whether there is a potential of value increase in the future. Therefore, Alan is interested in knowing the future planning of Camden, for example, the infrastructure to be built. Also, Alan is getting married with her girlfriend next year, they are planning to have a baby in near future. So Alan is also curious about the welfare and education of children in Camden.

Motivation & Goals
  • Find a new apartment in a great neighbourhood
  • Try to do the good decisions based on as much information as possible
